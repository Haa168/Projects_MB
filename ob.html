Objective to Caesar Cipher and RSA Encryption

Caesar Cipher RSA Encryption

Caesar Cipher

-Historical Use: The Caesar Cipher is named after Julius Caesar, who used it to encode his messages to his military. It was one of the earliest known encryption techniques.

-Educational Tool: It is often used in educational settings to introduce the concepts of encryption and decryption due to its simplicity.

-Puzzles and Games: The Caesar Cipher is frequently used in puzzles, games, and recreational cryptography.

RSA Encryption

-Secure Communication: RSA is widely used for securing communications over the internet, including emails, web traffic (HTTPS), and secure transactions.

-Digital Signatures: RSA is used to create digital signatures, which verify the authenticity and integrity of digital documents and software.

-Key Exchange: RSA is used in protocols like SSL/TLS for exchanging keys securely between parties.
