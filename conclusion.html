Conclusion

The journey from the Caesar Cipher to RSA encryption highlights the evolution of cryptography, from simple substitution techniques to complex algorithms that secure modern communications. Both methods, though vastly different in complexity and application, underscore the importance of encryption in safeguarding information throughout history and into the digital age.

Caesar Cipher

The Caesar Cipher is a classical encryption method, simple yet historically significant. Although it is easily broken today, it laid the foundation for understanding the basics of cryptography.

RSA Encryption

RSA encryption is a cornerstone of modern digital security, employing complex mathematical principles to ensure the confidentiality of communications in the digital age.
